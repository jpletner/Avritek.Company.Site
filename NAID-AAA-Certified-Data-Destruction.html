Secure Compliant Destruction Services.

Avritek maintains NAID AAA Certification which requires that we follow strict data security procedures at our San Diego facility that ensures that hard drives (HDDs) and other devices which contain proprietary or confidential information are handled properly on their way to resale, recycling or data destruction. Because we have extensive experience in hardware refreshes, recurring pickups, data center co-locations, and facility moves/closures, we are well prepared to handle any situation that carries with it an inherent security risk.


Avritek's secure data destruction services ensure compliance with FACTA, GLB, SOX, HIPAA, HITECH as well as all applicable local, state, and federal regulations.

Avritek provides destruction for hard drives, mobile devices, LTO tapes, and all other electronic storage media.
