FREE San Diego Electronics Recycling & Ewaste Pickups / Drop-offs

For FREE electronics recycling drop-offs please consult the "Items Accepted" panel to the right. We accept drop-offs at our facility Mon-Fri between the hours of 7:00am-3:30pm. Please call ahead if you have more than a few of the larger items on on the list.

For business clients in San Diego who need to recycle e-waste and electronics we offer recurring or project-based recycling services. Avritek does not export any hazardous materials overseas and follows all applicable local, state and federal guidlines when recycling materials. We hand disassemble non-hazardous materials at our facility to maximize the recoverable materials they contain, and pass these savings on to our clients.

We can typically offer FREE e-waste recycling pickups in San Diego to business clients who have 20 pieces that can be comprised of any of the following: network switches, servers, laptops, computers, monitors. After the minimum is met we will remove all additional e-waste at no extra charge, provided there is not more than ~1000lbs of scrap equipment in addition to the 20 item minimum (1000lbs is roughly equivilant to two large tri-wall cardboard bins stacked full). It is very rare that we have to charge after the 20 item minimum is met, and we will let know you in advance of your pickup if we think you might incur a service fee. If you do not meet the minimum, the average pickup fee is $50.

If you have frequent e-waste recycling diposal needs but don't always meet the minimum you might be a perfect candidate for our bin replacement services. Avritek will provide e-waste recycling containers at your facility which we will replace when needed. Usually we can provide this service at FREE of charge.
