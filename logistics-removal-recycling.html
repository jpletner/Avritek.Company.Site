Logistics, Removal, Recycling

We buy equipment!

We buy IT, lab/test, A/V, telecom, and medical equipment!


We got our start as an electronics equipment reseller over a decade ago and to this day it is a core part of what we do. Our expertise is in IT, lab/test, and medical equipment. We evaluate equipment of all varieties, in all conditions and quantities. Contact us today to see what we can offer for your equipment.




Responsible transportation and disposal.


Avritek provides logistics services for clients with technology equipment who need assurance that their equipment will be handled with care. We have extensive experience in data center deinstallations, high-tech facility moves and closures, and technology refreshes. We offer our nationwide logistics as a standalone service, or bundled with our destruction and asset recovery offerings. For clients who need to dispose of obsolete technology we offer regular or project-based technology recycling services.
